How It All Began

From A Small Passion Project To An Award-Winning Salon

Our founder Jessica Miller noticed that traditional pet grooming salons were often loud, rushed, and stressful for animals. She set out to build a calm, sanctuary-like environment where pets could relax during their grooming session.

Today, PawLuxe has grown into a state-of-the-art facility featuring private suites, hydrotherapy tubs, and acoustic dampening to deliver fear-free pampering.

Our Mission

To enhance the wellbeing and happiness of pets through fear-free handling, organic skincare products, and artistic grooming precision.

Our Vision

To set the global standard for luxury boutique pet grooming, where safety, science, and affection define every single pet visit.
